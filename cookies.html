Cookie Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, as well as to provide information to the owners of the site. Cookies can be "session" cookies (deleted when you close your browser) or "persistent" cookies (which remain on your device for a set period or until deleted).

2. How We Use Cookies

Restio Wellness uses cookies for the following purposes:

2.1 Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for the website to function. They enable core functionality such as remembering your cookie consent preferences. You cannot opt out of these cookies.

  • rw_cookie_consent — Stores your cookie consent decision. Duration: 365 days.

2.2 Analytics Cookies (with consent)

With your consent, we use analytics cookies to understand how visitors interact with our website. This helps us improve the site's content and usability.

  • Google Analytics (_ga, _gid, _gat) — Used to distinguish users and throttle request rates. Duration: up to 2 years. Provider: Google LLC.

Google Analytics data is anonymized where possible. You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

2.3 Advertising Cookies (with consent)

With your consent, we may display personalized ads via Google AdSense. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our site or other sites.

  • Google AdSense (IDE, DSID, FLC) — Used for ad targeting and measurement. Duration: up to 13 months. Provider: Google LLC.

You can manage your Google ad preferences at Google Ad Settings.

2.4 Amazon Affiliate Cookies

When you click an Amazon product link from our site, Amazon may place cookies on your device to track your purchase for our affiliate commission. These cookies are governed by Amazon's Cookie Policy.

3. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can manage your cookie preferences in several ways:

  • Our cookie banner: When you first visit our site, you can choose to accept or decline non-essential cookies.
  • Browser settings: Most browsers allow you to refuse or delete cookies. See your browser's help documentation for instructions.
  • Third-party opt-outs: Use the tools provided by Google (links above) to opt out of analytics and advertising cookies.
